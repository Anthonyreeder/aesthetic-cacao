Quick-Fire Trivia

What percentage of the ocean is explored?

Only about 5%. We know more about the surface of Mars than we do about our own ocean floor.

How many times does your heart beat per day?

About 100,000 times. That's 35 million times a year, and roughly 2.5 billion in a lifetime.

Which country has the most time zones?

France. Thanks to overseas territories, France spans 12 time zones. More than Russia.

What's the strongest muscle in the body?

The masseter (jaw muscle). It can exert up to 200 pounds of force on your molars.

How fast does a sneeze travel?

About 100 mph. That's faster than most cars on a motorway. Cover your mouth.
