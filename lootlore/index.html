Lore You Missed

Mario has killed thousands

If you count every Goomba, Koopa, and enemy across all games, Mario's body count is estimated at over 18,000.

The Companion Cube is alive

In Portal, GLaDOS hints that the Companion Cube can't talk. She never says it can't think. A dev confirmed it screams when incinerated.

Pokémon was nearly cancelled

Game Freak almost went bankrupt during development. Pokémon Red and Green took 6 years to make and was nearly scrapped twice.

The Zelda timeline makes no sense on purpose

Nintendo admitted they created the timeline AFTER the games, not before. The 'official' timeline is a retcon.

GTA San Andreas has a ghost

In the woods near Mt. Chiliad, players have reported seeing a ghostly figure. Rockstar never confirmed or denied it.
