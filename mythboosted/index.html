Myths: Busted or Confirmed?

Herobrine is in Minecraft - BUSTED

Every update says 'Removed Herobrine' as a joke. He was never in the code. But the legend won't die.

You can survive the nuke in CoD - CONFIRMED

In certain Call of Duty missions, specific spots let you survive nuclear blasts. Unintended but reproducible.

Mew is under the truck - BUSTED

The most persistent Pokémon myth. There's nothing under the truck near the S.S. Anne. But you CAN get Mew through a different glitch.

GTA has a Bigfoot - CONFIRMED (sort of)

After years of denial, Rockstar added an actual Bigfoot mission in GTA V as a nod to the myth from San Andreas.

Polybius exists - UNRESOLVED

The legendary arcade cabinet that caused nightmares and was collected by men in black. No cabinet has ever been found. But multiple witnesses exist.
