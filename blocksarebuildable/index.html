Did You Know?

Endermen speak English backwards

Record an Enderman's idle sounds, reverse the audio, and you'll hear distorted words like 'hello' and 'what's up.' Mojang confirmed this.

The world is 8x the size of Earth

A Minecraft world is 60 million blocks wide. That's over 60,000km, roughly 8 times the surface area of Earth.

Creepers were a coding accident

Notch was trying to make a pig model and accidentally swapped the height and length values. The result was so creepy he kept it.
