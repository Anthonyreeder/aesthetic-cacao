Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

Information We Collect

  • We use standard YouTube and social media analytics (views, watch time, demographics) provided by the platforms to improve our content.

YouTube API Services

  • Our applications use YouTube API Services to upload and manage video content on our channels.
  • We store OAuth tokens securely to maintain access to our own channels only.
  • We do not access, collect, or store any viewer data through the YouTube API.
  • You can revoke our application's access via Google Security Settings.
